Retention FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the average customer retention rate for repair shops?+

Most independent repair shops retain 45-60% of customers annually. Top-performing shops consistently achieve retention rates above 70% through proactive communication and maintenance reminders.

Why is customer retention important?+

Retaining an existing customer costs significantly less than acquiring a new one. Repeat customers also spend more over time and are more likely to refer friends and family.

What tools improve retention?+

Digital inspections, automated maintenance reminders, service history tracking, and SMS communication have the greatest impact on retention performance.

How often should shops communicate with customers?+

Industry leaders maintain contact every 3-6 months through maintenance reminders, follow-ups, and educational content without overwhelming customers.
