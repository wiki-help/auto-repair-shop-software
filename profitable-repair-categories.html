Repair Categories
Ranked by Gross Margin

01

Diagnostics & ADAS Calibration — 65-75% Gross Margin

The highest-margin work in the shop. Low parts cost, specialized tooling investment pays off quickly, and customers accept premium rates for complex electronic systems. Growing fastest due to vehicle technology adoption.

02

Electrical & Module Programming — 60-70% Gross Margin

Increasingly common as vehicles add complexity. Shops with OEM-level scan tools capture work that dealers can't always schedule quickly. High hourly rate and minimal parts costs drive excellent margins.

03

Brake Service — 55-65% Gross Margin

High customer urgency, predictable labor times, and consistent demand make brake service one of the most reliable margin contributors. Average ARO of $280-$420 with fast cycle time.

04

Transmission Service — 50-60% Gross Margin

Complex work that commands premium labor rates. Shops specializing in transmission see higher-than-average ARO ($650-$2,400) with strong margins on fluid and filter services at the lower end.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Which repair category has the highest profit margin?+

Diagnostic and ADAS calibration services consistently deliver the highest margins — often 65-75% gross margin — due to low parts costs and premium labor rates. Transmission service and electrical diagnostics also rank highly.

Are oil changes worth doing from a profit perspective?+

On their own, oil changes average only 20-30% gross margin. Their real value is as a gateway: shops that convert oil change customers to additional services see 2.8× higher ARO per visit, making them a critical retention and upsell tool.

Why do brake jobs rank so high in profitability?+

Brake service combines moderate parts costs (35-40%) with fast labor times and high customer urgency. Top shops achieve $280-$420 ARO on brake jobs with 55-65% gross margin, making them one of the best margin-per-hour categories.

How can I shift my shop's mix toward higher-margin work?+

Invest in diagnostic equipment to capture more ADAS and electrical work. Train advisors to identify and present high-value repair opportunities during every inspection. Shops that systematically pursue higher-margin categories add 4-7% to overall gross margin.
