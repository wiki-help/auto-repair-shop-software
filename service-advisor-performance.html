The 5 Patterns

What Top Advisors
Consistently Do

These five behaviors appear in the top 15% of service advisors across shops of all sizes. They're process-driven, not personality-driven — and the best auto repair software gives every advisor the tools to execute them consistently.

01

They Present Every Inspection Digitally

Top advisors never present a verbal estimate without the photo or video from the inspection. Every recommended service is shown, not just described. This single habit drives 2× higher approval rates.

02

They Explain Consequence, Not Just Cost

Average advisors say 'your brake pads are at 2mm — that's $280.' Top advisors say 'your brake pads are at 2mm — here's the photo. At this level, you're 2-3 weeks from metal-on-metal, which means rotors too. Today it's $280; next month it's likely $650.' Context drives decisions.

03

They Send Estimates via SMS, Not Phone Calls

Phone tag kills approval rates. Shops that follow up within 24 hours via SMS generate 32% more repeat business and achieve 3× faster estimate approval times. Top advisors default to text for estimates.

04

They Follow Up on Every Declined Item

Top advisors treat declined work as deferred, not lost. They log every declined service and follow up at 7, 30, and 90 days. This single habit adds $8,000-$15,000/month in recovered revenue per advisor.

05

They Own the Customer Relationship

High performers know their repeat customers by name, reference their vehicle's history proactively, and send maintenance reminders before the customer calls. Personalized service drives 40% higher retention rates.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What ARO should a top service advisor be achieving?+

Top service advisors consistently achieve $550-$750 ARO compared to the industry average of $390-$450. The difference comes from comprehensive digital inspections, photo-backed recommendations, and consistent presentation of all identified work — not pressure tactics.

What is a good estimate approval rate for a service advisor?+

Industry average approval rate is 58-65%. Top advisors achieve 78-88% approval rates by presenting estimates digitally with supporting photos, explaining the consequence of deferring, and offering clear pricing upfront.

How do you measure service advisor performance?+

Track ARO, approval rate, CSI score, upsell capture rate, and declined work follow-up conversion. The best advisors score in the top quartile across all five — not just ARO. High ARO with poor CSI scores is a warning sign.

How long does it take to develop a high-performing service advisor?+

With the right tools and training, advisors typically show measurable improvement within 60-90 days. Digital inspection platforms accelerate development by creating a consistent process, reducing reliance on experience and memory.
